Birria Recipe Page

Birra is a popular Mexican dish that originated in the Jalisco state. It is made with a varierty of ingredients that come together to create a delicious and fulfilling dish.

This authentic Mexican dish can be enjoyed as beef stew topped with onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of fresh lime juice or as the famous birria tacos!

Birria has gained so much popularity, and for good reason: it is really delicious!

Here are some essential ingredients to making Birria:

  • Dried Chiles: You need guajillo chiles, ancho chiles, and arbol chiles.
  • Spices and bay leaves: garlic, cumin, ground clove, Mexican oregano, Mexican cinnamon stick, black peppercorns, and 3 bay leaves.
  • Beef
  • Broth
  • Vinegar
  • Tomato, garlic, and onion

Finally, here are some key steps to creating the best Birria:

  1. Season your meat on both sides with salt and pepper. Then set a Dutch oven over medium-high heat with olive oil. Add the meat and sear on all sides until browned. Once all meat is seared, set aside. .
  2. In a medium pot, add all of the dried chiles, tomatoes, onion, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, and peppercorns, covering everything completely with water. Bring this to a boil, then lower the heat, cover, and cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Blend: Once the chiles are softened, transfer everything to a blender and add 1 cup of chile-soaked water. Add in the broth, vinegar, and the remaining spices. Blend until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  4. Strain the blended sauce into the pot with the seared meat (if necessary, depending on the power of your blender), gently stir, and bring to a boil. Bring heat to low, cover, and cook for 3-3 ½ hours, until meat is tender.
  5. Take the meat out to shred, and add shredded meat back to consumé.
  6. Add your desired toppings and enjoy!
