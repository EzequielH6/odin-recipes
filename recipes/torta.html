Torta De Chorizo

Torta de chorizo is loaded with Mexican chorizo and a variety of ingredients that come together to create a delicious and fulfilling sandwich.

The sandwich is typically served on a soft telera or bolillo roll and can be grilled or toasted before being filled with the ingredients.

This luscious sandwich is easy to make and can be served for breakfast, lunch, or dinner— anytime you need a quick, filling meal.

Here are some essential ingredients to making a Mexican Torta:

  • Sandwich roll
  • Mexican chorizo
  • Cheese
  • Refried beans
  • Toppings
  • Pickled jalapeños
  • Condiments

Finally, here are some key steps to creating the most delicious Mexican Torta:

  1. Heat a large skillet or a pan over medium heat and add the olive oil or any oil you prefer. Add the chorizo sausages and crumble the meat into the pan with the back of a wooden spoon.
  2. Cook stirring constantly until the flavorful pork chorizo is lightly crispy and cooked through (about 8 minutes).
  3. While the chorizo is cooking, slice the telera or bolillos rolls in half and lightly butter the crumb.
  4. Heat a griddle or a comal and toast the bread on all sides until slightly golden and crisp.
  5. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the crumb of each top piece of the rolls. Add a layer of refried beans on the crumb or each bottom piece.
  6. Once the chorizo is cooked, spoon it on top of the refried beans, then arrange a few slices of cheese if you want.
  7. Follow with lettuce, tomato slices, onions, avocado, and then jalapeño peppers. Season lightly with a sprinkle of salt.
  8. Close the sandwich and repeat the steps to assemble 3 more tortas and serve immediately or pack them for later.
